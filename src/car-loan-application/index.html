What to include

A useful request includes your income, employment, residence, down payment range, vehicle need, contact information and any credit context that matters. If you already have a trade-in, include the year, make, model, mileage and payoff if there is one. If you are replacing a vehicle because it is unreliable, explain the urgency. If the vehicle is for work, describe the job need.

Car Lender can review a request more clearly when the borrower explains the whole picture. The goal is not to make the application sound perfect. The goal is to make it accurate enough that the next step can be discussed without avoidable back and forth.
