The process

Four focused steps from first message to a real vehicle conversation.

  1. 01

    Define the need

    Start by explaining what the vehicle needs to do. A commuter car, family SUV, work truck and van are different requests. The use case affects payment comfort, insurance, maintenance and vehicle selection.

  2. 02

    Prepare the basics

    Gather identification, address, income proof, employment details, down payment range and trade-in information. If credit is challenged, prepare a short explanation of what happened and what has changed.

  3. 03

    Review payment fit

    The review should account for the loan payment plus insurance, fuel and normal ownership costs. A payment that leaves no margin is risky even when the borrower is approved.

  4. 04

    Move toward a vehicle

    Once the request has a practical path, the borrower can focus on a vehicle that matches the file. This protects the borrower from falling in love with inventory that does not fit the budget.
